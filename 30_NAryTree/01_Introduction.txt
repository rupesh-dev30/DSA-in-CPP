N-ary Tree Kya Hota Hai?

 - N-ary tree ek aisa tree hota hai jismein har node ke paas 'N' tak children ho sakte hain.
 - Agar N = 2 ho toh ye Binary Tree ban jata hai.
 - Par N-ary tree mein koi fixed limit nahi hoti, bas maximum N tak child ho sakte hain.

           1
        /  |  \
       2   3   4
      / \      |
     5   6     7

  Node 1 have 3 children
  Node 2 have 2 children
  Node 4 have 1 children
